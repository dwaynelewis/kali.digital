What is the price after a year
With our renewal price guarantee, we make sure your next renewal price is the same (even if the product price on the site goes up).
Why is your service based on a subscription plan? Can't I just make a one-time payment and be done with it?
The service we provide to our customers is part of the packaged deal that comes with the product we provide as a company. With your ongoing subscription you can rest assured that you not only have a state-of-the-art digital business card, but also a personalized online domain name, a server that we maintain monthly, a phone app that sends you automatic notifications, and unlimited customer support for any questions or changes you may wish to make.

How long until I get the digital business card?
Up to 5 business days from the time you fill in the required information following payment. You will also get our app which will help you never miss a customer!
Who do I turn to for questions and support?
